The Purpose of the dashboad is to investigate the sample of bacteria species. We analyze top 10 bacterial species that live in human Bellybutton. What this study based on is the behavior of the bacteria that is capable of producing proteins that taste like beef to start creating synthetic beef. This dashboard displays the test results of a collection of human belly buttons with different demographics.Each sample is refenced by an ID number, once choosen it will reveal the sample's metadata along with the test results.
